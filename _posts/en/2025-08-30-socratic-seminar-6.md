## Announcements

We are delighted to invite you to this month’s BitDevs Meetup in Cotonou!

Last month, we explored the different developer programs offered by [Btrust](https://btrust.tech) and [Bitshala](https://bitshala.org), along with an introductory workshop on BTCPay Server to understand the basics of its use.

This month, we are going even further.

We will connect our BTCPay Server instance to a web application and perform live Bitcoin payments together.

This will be the perfect opportunity to:

- Deepen your practical knowledge of Bitcoin payment integration

- Network with other developers, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts

- Ask your questions and actively participate in technical discussions

Don’t miss out on this opportunity!

Reserve your spot now and join the local BitDevs community for a session full of learning and hands-on practice.

We warmly thank our sponsor [Btrust](https://btrust.tech) for supporting the growth of Bitcoin Developers Community in Benin.
